About Wizard101

Wizard101 is a free-to-play MMORPG developed by KingsIsle Entertainment. You play as a young wizard enrolled in the Ravenwood School of Magical Arts, where you learn spells, fight monsters, complete quests, and explore magical worlds in the Spiral.

Magic Schools

  • Fire - Powerful over time spells with damage over time effects.
  • Ice - Defensive and tanky, great at absorbing damage.
  • Storm - High risk, high reward with massive damage.
  • Myth - Focused on summoning minions and unique strategies.
  • Life - Healers and support wizards.
  • Death - Drains enemy health while healing yourself.
  • Balance - A mix of all schools with powerful utility spells.
